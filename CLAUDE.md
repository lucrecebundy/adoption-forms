# Adoption Forms Express — Website Redesign

## What this project is

Redesign of adoptionformsexpress.com — a Nebraska stepparent adoption forms business owned by Lucrece H. Bundy, Esq. (licensed Nebraska adoption attorney). The redesign exists to convert better, rank higher in Google AND AI search (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews), and signal real attorney expertise — without creating UPL exposure.

## Non-negotiable constraints

- **UPL guardrails** in master doc Section 3 are non-negotiable. Read them every session.
- The site is built for **uncontested Nebraska stepparent adoption only**. Contested cases route to Bundy Law LLC.
- **Distinguish Adoption Forms Express (the product) from Bundy Law LLC (the affiliated law firm)** — they share a founder, not a legal entity.
- **Footer disclaimer required on every page** — exact text in master doc Section 3.
- **No language promising specific legal outcomes** ("guaranteed adoption," etc.)
- **Pricing is $299 (DIY) and $499 (Done-For-You)** — the live site currently has three different prices; this is being fixed.

## Session workflow

At the start of every session:

1. Read the master doc — especially Section 9 (Master Open Decisions) and Section 10 (Master Change Log) so you know what's TBD and what changed last session.
2. Read the page-level doc(s) for whatever we're working on.
3. Confirm scope with Tyler before making changes.

During the session:

- If a decision affects only one page, update that page's doc.
- If a decision affects the whole site (voice, design, SEO, stack), update the master.
- Don't invent new conventions — if something isn't in the spec, ask first.

At the end of every session:

1. Update the page-level doc's change log.
2. Update the master doc's change log (Section 10).
3. Update Master Open Decisions (Section 9) if anything got resolved or any new decision opened.
4. Update Site Architecture & Page Status (Section 2) if any page changed status.
5. Bump the version number on whichever docs were materially changed.
