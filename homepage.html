Step 1

Do you qualify for a Nebraska DIY stepparent adoption?

Adoption Forms Express is built for uncontested Nebraska stepparent adoptions. If your situation matches the list below, you're a great fit. If not, we'll route you to the right next step.

✓You are legally married to the child's biological parent
✓The child has lived with you for at least 6 months
✓You are at least 19 years old
✓You reside in Nebraska with the child and his/her biological parent
✓The other biological parent will consent, is deceased, or their parental rights have been terminated
✓Your case is uncontested
